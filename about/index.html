About Est. South East QLD

Homes, built with intent.

LDC Built is a Brisbane and Gold Coast residential builder focused on the homes in between — not so mass-market you feel like a number, not so custom you can't afford it.

We started LDC because too many South East Queensland families were choosing between two bad options: volume builders where the experience ends the day the contract is signed, or custom builders where the price doubles before you're out of the ground.

Our approach is simple. Fifteen carefully considered designs, fixed pricing you can trust, a single point of contact from enquiry through handover, and trades we work with on every project — not whoever was available.

We work across Brisbane and the Gold Coast exclusively, which means our site supervisors are never more than a short drive from your build.

01 How we work

Three principles.

Value 01

Fixed price means fixed.

Our contracts are genuinely fixed. Site costs, inclusions, allowances — all locked in before you sign. No "provisional sums" that blow out the budget twelve weeks in.

Value 02

One point of contact.

From first enquiry through to handover, you speak to the same person. No being passed around sales, drafting, contracts, and construction as the build progresses.

Value 03

Trades we actually know.

We work with the same core trades on every build. That consistency shows up in the finish, the timelines, and the way things get resolved when issues come up.
