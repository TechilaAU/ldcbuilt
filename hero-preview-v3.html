About LDC Built

Built by nature,
shaped by design.

We believe the home you live in should be a considered reflection of the life you want to live — not a compromise between what's available and what's affordable.

Since 2014, LDC Built has been delivering residential homes across Brisbane and the Gold Coast — carefully considered designs, fixed-price contracts, and a single point of contact from first enquiry through to handover. Our portfolio spans efficient first-home footprints through to architectural family residences, each built to the same exacting standard.

We work across two regions exclusively, which means our site supervisors are never more than a short drive from your build. It's a deliberate constraint — and one of the reasons our homes get delivered on time, on budget, and to the standard that earns us 42% repeat clients.
