02 How we build

A simpler four-step.

Step 01

Consult

An initial conversation about your block, your brief, and your budget. No pressure — just a clear starting point.

Step 02

Design

Select from fifteen fixed-price designs, or tailor one to your site. We handle the drawings, approvals, and engineering.

Step 03

Contract

Transparent fixed-price contracts with a detailed inclusions list. No surprises once the build begins.

Step 04

Build

An average 18-week program with weekly site updates, a dedicated site supervisor, and a single point of contact.
